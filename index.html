About Us

Ekklesia was founded as a platform that removes the traditional barriers to therapy and make mental health care more accessible to christians. This is done by providing professional, affordable, and personalized therapy in a convenient online format. Ekklesia's network of licensed practicioners are there to help millions of christians take ownership of their mental health and work towards their personal goals. As the unmet need for mental health services continues to grow, Ekklesia is committed to expanding access to therapy globally.
